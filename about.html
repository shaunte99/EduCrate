Powering the Future of Education for Students, Parents, and Schools

Our Mission

EduCrate is revolutionizing education by connecting students, teachers, parents, and schools in one smart, scalable system.

The EduCrate Student Card

Every student receives a physical EduCrate card. Attendance is logged the moment they enter school, and parents get real-time notifications. Grades, achievements, and participation are all tracked — creating a complete learning passport.

Parent Transparency

Parents can instantly see attendance, performance, and teacher feedback, keeping them fully connected to their child’s academic journey.
